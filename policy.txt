MAJU Admission Dataset 2026
1. GENERAL ADMISSION INFORMATION

University: Mohammad Ali Jinnah University (MAJU), Karachi.

Status: HEC Recognized.

Admission Cycle: Spring and Fall.

Application Method: Online via the MAJU Admission Portal or on-campus visit.

Processing Fee: PKR 3,000 (Non-refundable).

Admission Fee: PKR 20,000 (One-time, non-refundable).

3. FEE STRUCTURE (SPRING 2026)

Tuition Fee (Undergraduate): PKR 9,000 per credit hour.

Tuition Fee (Graduate/MS): PKR 9,000 per credit hour.

Tuition Fee (PhD): PKR 11,000 per credit hour.

Taxes: 5% withholding tax applicable if the annual fee exceeds PKR 200,000 (Adjustable).

4. SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AID

Merit-Based (New Students - Undergraduate):

85% or above in Intermediate: 100% Tuition Fee Waiver.

80% to 84.9%: 75% Tuition Fee Waiver.

75% to 79.9%: 50% Tuition Fee Waiver.

Merit-Based (Graduate):

3.50 CGPA or 80% marks: 50% Tuition Fee Waiver.

3.00 to 3.49 CGPA: 25% Tuition Fee Waiver.

Other Categories:

Need-Based: Up to 100% (Subject to interview/financial assessment).

Kinship/Sibling: 25% waiver for siblings of current students.

Alumni: 25% to 100% waiver for MAJU/PGC graduates.

Corporate/Professional: Waivers for members of ACCA, ICMAP, and PIPFA.

Continuation Criteria: Students must maintain a minimum CGPA (typically 2.50 for BS, 3.00 for MS) and a full course load to keep scholarships.
